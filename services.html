Our Services

In restoring the past and building the future, we have a wealth of services and here is an introduction to all.

Design and Planning

We help people design and create the inside of their home to reflect and express the uniqueness.

The client knows what they want, what they like and they can see their project in their mind’s eye. We help the client to bring it from inside their head into reality, and we do it such a way the client knows they are always in charge even as we advise against something that might not work, in finding compromises in both design and cost, the client is always in charge. We aim to please so that the client can enjoy their home in the way they envision.

Construction Services

We plan and design new constructions and renovations to be functional and aesthetically pleasing.

From pre-construction services where the practical aspects of the projects are analysed to construction management, planning and execution phase through to post-construction phase, we are with the client the whole way.
